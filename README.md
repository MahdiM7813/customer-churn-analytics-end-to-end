# 📊 Customer Churn Analysis (End-to-End Project)

## 🎯 Project Overview
This project analyzes customer churn behavior in a telecom company using Python, SQL, and Power BI. The goal is to identify key drivers of churn, segment customers based on risk and value, and provide actionable business insights for retention strategies.

---

## 🧠 Business Problem
Customer churn directly impacts revenue and long-term business sustainability. This analysis focuses on answering:

- Which customers are likely to churn?
- What are the main drivers of churn?
- Which customer segments are most valuable and at risk?

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- Python (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn)
- SQL (PostgreSQL)
- Power BI (Dashboards)
- Jupyter Notebook

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 📌 Contract Type
Month-to-month customers show significantly higher churn rates compared to long-term contracts.

### 📌 Customer Tenure
New customers (0–12 months) are the most likely to churn, indicating a critical early lifecycle risk.

### 📌 Payment Method
Electronic check users demonstrate higher churn probability compared to automated payment methods.

### 📌 Service Usage
Customers without Tech Support and Online Security are more likely to churn.

### 📌 Segmentation Insight
High Risk + High Value customers represent the most critical revenue-at-risk segment.
